About us
Teachers are the makers of a nation. The New Education Policy, 2003 has in unequivocal terms reiterated the need for an effective system of Teacher Education. Recognizing the growing necessity and increasing importance of quality Teacher Education to meetthe challenges of 21st century, Soghra college of teacher education, KondaBheemanapally(Village), Devarakonda(Mandal), Nalgonda(District), Telangana (State) has established Soghra college of teacher education (B.Ed) to prepare teachers of today & tomorrow. The chairman of the society is M.A. Khaleel. He has been in the field of education for the last 20 years imparting quality education to the children in the backward localities.


OUR MISSION

Soghra college of teacher education (B.Ed) strives to provide an educational foundation compromising of a judicious mix of theoretical and practical training to help teacher trainees develop the appropriate skills, knowledge, attitude and character needed to become effective and fruitful teachers in their respective fields. B. Ed. Courses are designed to produced we informed, efficient and self-reliant teachers, capable of effective contribution to the development efforts in the rural and urban areas of India through the education sector. Soghra college of teacher education (B.Ed) is dedicated to provide quality training to the enrolled students and inspire on them qualities of leadership, creativity and innovation. .
